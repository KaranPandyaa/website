ReQuBis: Reconfigurable Qudrupedal Bipedal Snake Robots

Harshad Zade, Aadesh Varude, Karan Pandya, Ajinkya Kamat, Shital Chiddarwar, Rohan Thakker.
arXiv, 2021
/ video / arXiv

Reconfigurable Quadrupedal-Bipedal Snake Robots (ReQuBiS), can transform between mobility modes of a snake robot and a quadruped robot without rearranging modules. Hence, achieving the best of both worlds.
